Welcome to Forget Me Not!

A reminder app for warranties, subscriptions and more!

The purpose of this application is to provide a single source of truth that holds and tracks all of a user's warranties, accounts with recurring payments, and similar subscriptions. The user will be able to upload products and keep them organized so that nothing falls through the cracks. Forget-Me-Not notifies the user before the end date or renewal date of their various subscriptions. Never pay for an abandoned service again!


  • Simple, free, and easy to use
  • Timely reminders with no hassle
  • Safe and secure accounts and data
  • Easy uploads through an intuitive form
